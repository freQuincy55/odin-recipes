Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Homemade chocolate chip cookies. These cookies are soft and chewy.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup (2 sticks) melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp almond extract
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1-2 cups milk chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 350 F
  2. Mix together white sugar, brown sugar, and butter until smooth
  3. Add eggs, beat on medium speed for 2 minutes
  4. Mix in vanilla, almond extract, salt, and baking soda
  5. Slowy add flour, then slowly add desired amount of chocolate chips
  6. Scoop dough onto baking sheet, bake for 8-10 minutes
